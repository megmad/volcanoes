Active Volcanoes

Volcanoes are a very interesting topic. Many people may be scared but there is not much to worry about. Most of the volcanoes on this planet are not active and even the ones that are, don't erupt very often, and even when they do, they dont have enormous explosions like the ones we see in movies. Although most volcanoes are not very scary, there are still a few that are like the ones we imagine when we think of a volcano. This is what we are going to talk about today.


Mauna Loa

Mauna loa is the largest active volcano on planet Earth. It is located on the Island of Hawai'i which is the largest island in Hawaii. The last time this volcano erupted was April 15, 1984.The last major eruption was in 1950. This eruption destroyed villages and many lives were lost. This volcano is very clearly seen from satalite images.
